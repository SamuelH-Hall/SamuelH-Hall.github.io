Automated Sorter Specialist

For my internship, I accepted a positon at the United Parcel Service as an Automated Sorter Specialist. This is an incredibly unique positon as it helped me gather more knowledge of the systems within UPS as a whole. Many of the job roles required quick learning of various tools used to track an report data for many of the business processes that UPS has built through there years of operation. I feel this helped me gained a greater understanding of the technology in place at UPS and how different systems worked with one another to accomplish various task through the operation.

Responsibilities included:
  • Monitoring SCADA system for issues that arise within the system
  • Communicating with the employees on the floor of issues and record the issue within excel while keeping track of the times
  • Operating in the FlexOps interface to manage the movement of package flow for the posisorters
  • Critically think of plans to adjust through a high impact operational issue that may not be resolved in a timely manner
  • Understanding the effects of every action you commit and how it will impact others within the system
  • Tracking of data for various different needs of the operation to ensure their best chance of success
  • Ability to multitask in a high capacity to be able to juggle different responsibilities through the operation
  • Positive attitude towards motivating the success of the operation
